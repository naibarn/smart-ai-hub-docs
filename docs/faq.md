# Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

### What is Smart AI Hub?

Smart AI Hub is an AI-powered workflow automation platform that enables you to build, deploy, and manage intelligent agents at scale.

### Is Smart AI Hub free?

We offer a free tier with limited features. For production use, please check our [pricing page](https://smart-ai-hub.com/pricing).

### What LLM providers are supported?

We support OpenAI, Anthropic Claude, Google Gemini, and more. See our [LLM Engine documentation](./llm/) for the full list.

## Technical Questions

### How do I create my first agent?

Check out our [Getting Started Guide](./getting-started/) for a step-by-step tutorial.

### Can I integrate with my existing systems?

Yes! Smart AI Hub supports 100+ integrations through MCP (Model Context Protocol). See our [Integrations documentation](./integrations/).

### Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We implement enterprise-grade security with RBAC, encryption, and compliance. See our [Security documentation](./security/).

## Billing & Support

### How do I contact support?

Email us at support@smart-ai-hub.com or open an issue on [GitHub](https://github.com/naibarn/Smart-AI-Hub).

### Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings.
